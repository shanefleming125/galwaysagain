Galway Ireland
A Lovely Musical Town

Galway, located in West Ireland, is one of the Ireland's prized possessions. Every year, thousands of tourists flock to Galway to enjoy traditional Irish Food and Music. You a certain to find quaint irish pubs, fantastic Irish music, and the most kindest people on the planet. The ocean air is sure to wake you up after several pints of Guinness, and you are sure to emjoy the freshest seafood Ireland has to offer. As you take a stroll the Quay St on a summer's night, you are sure to encounter street performs from accross Europe and Ireland. Many artists and musicians make their way to Galway every summer to performs for tourists. The street culture here is amazing and mind blowing!
